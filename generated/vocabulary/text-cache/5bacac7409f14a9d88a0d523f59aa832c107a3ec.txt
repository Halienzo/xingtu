Unit 2 Home sweet home
一、语法考点
（一）Can/Could 引导的请求与许可句型
1. 表示请求
①结构：Can/Could you please + 动词原形 + 其他？其中，Could you
please...? 比 Can you please...? 语气更委婉。
②回答：肯定回答常用 “Sure./Of course./No problem.” 等；否定回答常用
“Sorry, but...” 并说明理由。例如：Could you please sweep the floor?（你能
扫一下地吗？）Sure. I'll do it right away.（当然，我马上就做。）
2. 表示请求许可
①结构：Can/Could I + 动词原形 + 其他？同样，Could I...? 语气更委婉。
②回答：肯定回答用 “Yes, you can.”；否定回答用 “Sorry, you can't.” 或
“Sorry, but...”。例如：Could I hang out with my friends after school?（放学
后 我 能 和 朋 友 们 出 去 玩 吗 ？ ） Sorry, you can't. You have to finish your
homework first.（抱歉，你不能。你得先完成作业。）
（二）一般现在时表将来
在一些表示位移的动词（如 go, come, leave, arrive 等）中，常用一般现
在时表示按计划、安排即将发生的动作，通常会有表示将来的时间状语。例如：
The train leaves at 6:30 tomorrow morning.（火车明天早上 6:30 发车。）
二、词汇考点
（一）重点单词
1. chore：可数名词，意为 “杂务；家务劳动” ，常用复数形式 chores。例
如：I have a lot of chores to do on weekends.（我周末有很多家务要做。）

-- 1 of 12 --

2. sweep：动词，意为 “打扫；清扫”，过去式为 swept。常见短语 sweep the
floor（扫地）。例如：My mother asks me to sweep the floor every day.
（妈妈让我每天扫地。）
3. floor：名词，意为 “地板；地面” 。也可表示 “楼层” ，英式英语中与序
数词连用时，the 常置于序数词前；美式英语中 the 常省略。例如：There
is a book on the floor.（地板上有一本书。）The library is on the second
floor.（图书馆在二楼。）
4. borrow：动词，意为 “借；借用” ，常用结构 borrow sth. from sb.（从
某人那里借某物） 。例如：I often borrow books from the library.（我经
常从图书馆借书。）
5. lend：动词，意为 “借给；借出” ，常用结构 lend sth. to sb. = lend sb. sth.
（把某物借给某人） 。例如：Can you lend your pen to me? = Can you
lend me your pen?（你能把你的钢笔借给我吗？）
6. invite：动词，意为 “邀请” ，名词形式为 invitation。常见短语 invite sb.
to do sth.（邀请某人做某事）；invite sb. to + 地点（邀请某人去某地） 。
例如：I want to invite my friends to my birthday party.（我想邀请我的朋
友们参加我的生日派对。）
7. prepare：动词，意为 “使做好准备；把…… 准备好” ，常见短语 prepare
for（为…… 做准备）；prepare sth.（准备某物） 。例如：We are preparing
for the exam.（我们正在为考试做准备。）
8. reason：可数名词，意为 “原因；理由” 。例如：The reason for his being
late is that he missed the bus.（他迟到的原因是错过了公交车。）

-- 2 of 12 --

9. neighbor：可数名词，意为 “邻居” 。例如：My neighbor is very kind.
（我的邻居非常友善。）
10. care：动词，意为 “在意；担忧；关心” ，常见短语 care about（关心；
在意）；care for（照顾；照料） 。例如：We should care about the
environment.（我们应该关心环境。）
（二）重点短语
1. help out：意为 “帮助分担工作、解决难题等” 。例如：I often help out with
housework at home.（我在家经常帮忙做家务。）
2. in order to：意为 “目的是；为了” ，后接动词原形，引导目的状语。例
如：He gets up early in order to catch the early bus.（他早起是为了赶上
早班车。）
3. depend on：意为 “依靠；信赖” 。例如：Children should not depend too
much on their parents.（孩子们不应该过于依赖他们的父母。）
4. take care of：意为 “照顾；照料” ，等同于 look after。例如：We should
take care of the old.（我们应该照顾老人。）
5. as a result：意为 “结果；因此” ，常位于句首，并用逗号与句子其他部
分隔开。例如：He didn't study hard. As a result, he failed the exam.（他
学习不努力。结果，他考试不及格。）

1. — Could you please ________ the floor? It's dirty.
— Sure.
A. sweeping B. sweep C. to sweep D. swept
答案：B
解析：Could you please 后接动词原形，所以选 B。
2. — Can I ________ your bike?
— Sorry, I've lent it to Tom.
A. borrow B. lend C. keep D. buy
答案：A
解析：根据回答可知是询问能否借对方的自行车，borrow 表示 “借入”，lend

解析：此处是请求许可，询问是否能邀请朋友参加派对，用 Can I...? 句型，
所以选 C。

（三）阅读理解
When we talk about home, we often think of its warmth and comfort. A
home is not just a building where we live; it's a place full of love and care.
In my family, we all do our part to make our home a better place. My
mother always keeps the house clean. She sweeps the floor and cleans the
rooms every day. She also cooks delicious meals for us. My father often helps
with the heavier chores. He takes out the rubbish and fixes things around the
house when they are broken.
As for me, I usually help with some small chores. I feed the fish and water
the plants. Sometimes, I also help my mother set the table before dinner. On
weekends, my parents and I often clean up the house together. We work as a
team, and it makes our home look tidy and beautiful.

We also like to decorate our home. We hang up photos of our family on the
walls. These photos show our happy moments together. We also put some
flowers in the living room. They make the house smell nice and feel more
comfortable.
Home is where we feel safe and loved. We should all do something to
make our homes better.
1. ( )What does the writer think of home?
A. It's just a building.
B. It's a place full of love and comfort.
C. It's only a place to live.
D. It's a place with many chores.
答案：B
解析：根据文章第一段 “A home is not just a building where we live; it's a
place full of love and care.” 可知，作者认为家是充满爱和关怀的地方，B 选
项正确。A 选项 “只是一座建筑” 和 C 选项 “只是居住的地方” 与原文不符；
D 选项 “有很多家务的地方” 不是作者对家的主要看法，所以选 B。
2. ( )What does the writer's mother do at home?
A. She takes out the rubbish.
B. She fixes things around the house.
C. She keeps the house clean and cooks meals.
D. She feeds the fish and waters the plants.
答案：C
解 析 ： 从 文 章 第 二 段 “My mother always keeps the house clean. She
sweeps the floor and cleans the rooms every day. She also cooks delicious
meals for us.” 可知，作者的妈妈保持房子干净并且做饭，C 选项正确。A 选
项 “倒垃圾” 和 B 选项 “修理房子周围的东西” 是作者爸爸做的；D 选项
“喂鱼和浇花” 是作者做的，所以选 C。
