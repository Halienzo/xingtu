Unit 6 Plan for Yourself

一、语法考点

（一）be going to 结构

含义：表示打算、计划或准备要做某事，也可表示根据现有迹象推测即将发生的事情。

构成：be（am/is/are）+ going to + 动词原形。例如：I am going to visit my grandparents this weekend.（我打算这个周末去看望我的祖父母。）It's going to rain. Look at the dark clouds.（要下雨了。看那些乌云。）

一般疑问句：将 be 动词提到主语前。肯定回答：Yes, 主语 + be. 否定回答：No, 主语 + be not. 例如：Are you going to play basketball after school?（你放学后打算打篮球吗？）Yes, I am.（是的，我打算打。）No, I'm not.（不，我不打算打。）

特殊疑问句：特殊疑问词 + be + 主语 + going to + 动词原形 + 其他？例如：What are you going to do tomorrow?（你明天打算做什么？）

（二）want to do sth. 结构

含义：表示 “想要做某事”，表达主语的意愿或想法。

用法：want 后接动词不定式作宾语。例如：I want to be a teacher when I grow up.（我长大后想成为一名教师。）She wants to buy a new dress.（她想买一条新裙子。）

二、词汇考点

（一）重点单词

engineer：n. 工程师。常见短语：computer engineer（计算机工程师），mechanical engineer（机械工程师）。例如：My brother is an engineer. He designs buildings.（我哥哥是一名工程师。他设计建筑物。）

pilot：n. 飞行员。例如：He always dreams of being a pilot and flying in the sky.（他一直梦想成为一名飞行员，在天空中翱翔。）

violinist：n. 小提琴手。由 violin（小提琴） + -ist（表示 “人” 的后缀）构成。例如：The violinist played a beautiful piece of music.（这位小提琴手演奏了一首美妙的乐曲。）

driver：n. 驾驶员；司机。drive（驾驶）的名词形式。例如：My father is a driver. He drives a bus.（我父亲是一名司机。他开公交车。）

pianist：n. 钢琴家。由 piano（钢琴） + -ist（表示 “人” 的后缀）构成。例如：Lang Lang is a famous pianist.（郎朗是一位著名的钢琴家。）

scientist：n. 科学家。science（科学）的名词形式，-ist 表示 “人”。例如：Edison was a great scientist.（爱迪生是一位伟大的科学家。）

college：n. 学院；大学；高等专科学校。例如：He is going to college next year.（他明年要上大学。）

education：n. 教育。educate（教育）的名词形式。例如：Education is very important for a country.（教育对一个国家非常重要。）

medicine：n. 药；医学。常见短语：take medicine（吃药）。例如：You should take the medicine three times a day.（你应该一天吃三次药。）

university：n. 综合性大学。例如：He studies in Peking University.（他在北京大学学习。）

（二）重点短语

grow up：长大；成熟；成长。例如：What do you want to be when you grow up?（你长大后想成为什么？）

be going to：打算；计划。后接动词原形，表示将来的计划或意图。例如：I'm going to visit my aunt this weekend.（我打算这个周末去看望我的姑姑。）

computer programmer：计算机程序设计员；编程人员。例如：My cousin wants to be a computer programmer.（我的表弟想成为一名计算机程序设计员。）

take acting lessons：上表演课。例如：She is going to take acting lessons to become an actress.（她打算上表演课，以便成为一名女演员。）

study medicine：学医。例如：He is going to study medicine at a university.（他打算在大学里学医。）

make sure：确保；查明。后常接从句或 of 短语。例如：Make sure you turn off the lights before you leave.（离开前确保你关掉了灯。）

be good at：擅长；在…… 方面做得好。后接名词、代词或动词 -ing 形式。例如：She is good at dancing.（她擅长跳舞。）

keep on doing sth.：继续做某事。例如：Keep on studying hard, and you will get good grades.（继续努力学习，你会取得好成绩的。）

take up：开始从事；占用（时间、空间等）。例如：I want to take up a new hobby.（我想培养一项新爱好。）

write down：写下；记录下。例如：Please write down your name and address.（请写下你的姓名和地址。）

（三）阅读理解

梦想职业与计划

In our class, everyone has a dream job. Li Ming wants to be a computer programmer. He is good at math and enjoys writing computer programs. He is going to study computer science at a university. He plans to get a part - time job to make some money and buy a good computer. He believes that with a good computer, he can practice writing programs better.

Wang Mei dreams of being a teacher. She loves children and is patient with them. She is going to study education at college. She also wants to get some teaching experience by working as a volunteer in a primary school during her holidays. She hopes to be a popular teacher in the future.

Zhang Hua wants to be a doctor. He thinks doctors can help sick people. He is going to study medicine at a medical university. After graduating, he plans to work in a big hospital to gain more experience. He also wants to learn some foreign languages so that he can read medical books from other countries.

（ ）What does Li Ming want to be?A. A teacher. B. A doctor.C. A computer programmer. D. A volunteer.

（ ）How is Li Ming going to achieve his dream?A. By studying education at college.B. By studying computer science at a university and buying a good computer.C. By working in a big hospital.D. By working as a volunteer in a primary school.

（ ）Why does Wang Mei want to be a teacher?A. Because she is good at math.B. Because she likes helping sick people.C. Because she loves children and is patient with them.D. Because she wants to make a lot of money.

（ ）Where does Zhang Hua plan to work after graduating?A. In a big hospital. B. In a primary school.C. In a computer company. D. In a foreign country.
