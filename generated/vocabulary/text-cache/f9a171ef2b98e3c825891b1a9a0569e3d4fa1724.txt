Unit 7 When Tomorrow Comes

一、语法考点

（一）一般将来时

含义：表示将来某个时间要发生的动作或存在的状态，也表示将来经常或反复发生的动作。

构成形式

①will + 动词原形：常用来表示纯粹的将来，含有 “预见”“意愿” 等含义。例如：I will go to Beijing tomorrow.（我明天将去北京。）He will help you if you ask him.（如果你请求他，他会帮助你。）

②be going to + 动词原形：表示打算、计划或准备要做某事，也可表示根据现有迹象推测即将发生的事情 。例如：She is going to visit her grandparents this weekend.（她打算这个周末去看望她的祖父母。）Look at the clouds. It's going to rain.（看那些云。要下雨了。）

一般将来时的句型转换

①肯定句：主语 + will/be going to + 动词原形 + 其他。例如：They will play basketball after school.（他们放学后将打篮球。）He is going to buy a new book.（他打算买一本新书。）

②否定句：主语 + won't/be not going to + 动词原形 + 其他。例如：I won't go shopping tomorrow.（我明天不去购物。）She isn't going to visit her aunt.（她不打算去看望她的姑姑。）

③一般疑问句：Will + 主语 + 动词原形 + 其他？肯定回答：Yes, 主语 + will. 否定回答：No, 主语 + won't. 例如：Will you go to the park tomorrow?（你明天会去公园吗？）Yes, I will.（是的，我会去。）No, I won't.（不，我不会去。）

④特殊疑问句：特殊疑问词 + will + 主语 + 动词原形 + 其他？特殊疑问词 + be + 主语 + going to + 动词原形 + 其他？例如：What will you do tomorrow?（你明天打算做什么？）Where are you going to visit?（你打算去哪里参观？）

（二）There be 句型的一般将来时

结构：There will be... 或 There is/are going to be...，表示 “将会有……”。

区别：There will be 更强调客观上将来会有；There is/are going to be 表示根据计划、安排或迹象推测将来会有 。例如：There will be a meeting tomorrow.（明天将会有一个会议。）There are going to be more trees in our school.（我们学校将会有更多的树。）

句型转换

①否定句：There won't be.../ There isn't/aren't going to be... 例如：There won't be a party tonight.（今晚不会有派对。）There aren't going to be any books on the desk.（桌子上不会有任何书。）

②一般疑问句：Will there be...? / Is/Are there going to be...? 例如：Will there be a sports meeting next week?（下周会有运动会吗？）Are there going to be more people in the city?（城市里将会有更多的人吗？）

二、词汇考点

（一）重点单词

future：n. 将来；未来。常见短语：in the future（在将来）；in future（今后；从今以后）。例如：I want to be a doctor in the future.（我将来想成为一名医生。）You should be more careful in future.（你今后应该更小心。）

pollution：n. 污染；污染物。pollute（v. 污染）的名词形式。例如：Air pollution is a big problem.（空气污染是个大问题。）

environment：n. 环境。例如：We should protect our environment.（我们应该保护我们的环境。）

planet：n. 行星。例如：The earth is a planet.（地球是一颗行星。）

peace：n. 和平。例如：We all love peace.（我们都热爱和平。）

astronaut：n. 宇航员；航天员。例如：He dreams of being an astronaut.（他梦想成为一名宇航员。）

apartment：n. 公寓套房。例如：They live in an apartment.（他们住在一套公寓里。）

rocket：n. 火箭。例如：Rockets can send satellites into space.（火箭能把卫星送入太空。）

space：n. 太空；空间。例如：We can see many stars in space.（我们能在太空中看到许多星星。）

human：n. 人；人类。复数形式为 humans。例如：Humans should live in harmony with nature.（人类应该与自然和谐相处。）

（二）重点短语

in the future：在将来。例如：What will you do in the future?（你将来会做什么？）

live to be...：活到…… 岁。例如：My grandfather lived to be 90 years old.（我的祖父活到了 90 岁。）

hundreds of：许多；大量；成百上千。例如：There are hundreds of books in the library.（图书馆里有数百本书。）

fall down：突然倒下；跌倒；倒塌。例如：Be careful not to fall down.（小心别摔倒。）

look for：寻找。强调寻找的动作。例如：I'm looking for my keys.（我在找我的钥匙。）

fly to...：飞向；飞往。例如：He will fly to Shanghai tomorrow.（他明天将飞往上海。）

in danger：处于危险之中。例如：Many animals are in danger.（许多动物处于危险之中。）

over and over again：多次；反复地。例如：He read the story over and over again.（他反复读这个故事。）

get bored：感到厌烦。例如：I get bored when I watch the same movie again.（当我再次看同一部电影时，我感到厌烦。）

wake up：醒来。例如：I usually wake up at 6:30 in the morning.（我通常早上 6:30 醒来。）

（三）阅读理解

未来的生活

In the future, life will be very different. People will live in houses that can change colors and shapes according to the weather and their mood. These houses will be very comfortable and energy - saving. They will use solar energy to heat and cool the rooms, so there will be less pollution.

Transportation will also change a lot. People won't drive cars as often as they do now. Instead, they will use flying cars or high - speed trains. Flying cars will be able to take off and land vertically (垂直地), so they won't need long runways like airplanes. High - speed trains will be much faster than the trains we have now, and they will be more comfortable.

In the future, people will work at home more often. They will use computers and the Internet to communicate with their colleagues. Schools will also be different. Students won't need to go to school every day. They can study at home through online courses. They will be able to choose the courses they like and learn at their own pace.

However, there will also be some problems. For example, with more and more robots doing jobs, some people may lose their jobs. And if people don't go out often, they may become less healthy. So, we need to find ways to solve these problems to make the future life better.
