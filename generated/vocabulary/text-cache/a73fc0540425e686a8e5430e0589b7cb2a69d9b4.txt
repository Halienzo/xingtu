Unit 7 When Tomorrow Comes
一、语法考点
（一）一般将来时
1. 含义：表示将来某个时间要发生的动作或存在的状态，也表示将来经常或
反复发生的动作。
2. 构成形式
① 常用来表示纯粹的将来，含有 “预见”“意愿” 等
含义。例如：I will go to Beijing tomorrow.（我明天将去北京。）He will
help you if you ask him.（如果你请求他，他会帮助你。）
② 表示打算、计划或准备要做某事，也可
表示根据现有迹象推测即将发生的事情 。例如：She is going to visit
her grandparents this weekend.（她打算这个周末去看望她的祖父母。）
Look at the clouds. It's going to rain.（看那些云。要下雨了。）
3. 一般将来时的句型转换
① 主语 + will/be going to + 动词原形 + 其他。例如：They
will play basketball after school.（他们放学后将打篮球。）He is going
to buy a new book.（他打算买一本新书。）
② 主语 + won't/be not going to + 动词原形 + 其他。例如：
I won't go shopping tomorrow.（我明天不去购物。）She isn't going to
visit her aunt.（她不打算去看望她的姑姑。）

（二）There be 句型的一般将来时
1. 结构：There will be... 或 There is/are going to be...，表示 “将会有……”。
2. 区别：There will be 更强调客观上将来会有；There is/are going to be 表
示根据计划、安排或迹象推测将来会有 。例如：There will be a meeting
tomorrow.（明天将会有一个会议。）There are going to be more trees in
our school.（我们学校将会有更多的树。）
3. 句型转换
There won't be.../ There isn't/aren't going to be... 例如：

to be any books on the desk.（桌子上不会有任何书。）

二、词汇考点
（一）重点单词
1. n. 将来；未来。常见短语：in the future（在将来）；in future
（今后；从今以后）。例如：I want to be a doctor in the future.（我将
来想成为一名医生。）You should be more careful in future.（你今后应
该更小心。）
2. n. 污染；污染物。pollute（v. 污染）的名词形式。例如：Air
pollution is a big problem.（空气污染是个大问题。）
3. n. 环境。例如：We should protect our environment.（我
们应该保护我们的环境。）
4. n. 行星。例如：The earth is a planet.（地球是一颗行星。）
5. n. 和平。例如：We all love peace.（我们都热爱和平。）
6. n. 宇航员；航天员。例如：He dreams of being an astronaut.
（他梦想成为一名宇航员。）
7. n. 公寓套房。例如：They live in an apartment.（他们住在
一套公寓里。）
8. n. 火箭。例如：Rockets can send satellites into space.（火箭能
把卫星送入太空。）
9. n. 太空；空间。例如：We can see many stars in space.（我们能
在太空中看到许多星星。）

10. n. 人；人类。复数形式为 humans。例如：Humans should live
in harmony with nature.（人类应该与自然和谐相处。）
（二）重点短语
1. 在将来。例如：What will you do in the future?（你将来
会做什么？）
2. 活到…… 岁。例如：My grandfather lived to be 90 years old.
（我的祖父活到了 90 岁。）
3. 许多；大量；成百上千。例如：There are hundreds of books
in the library.（图书馆里有数百本书。）
4. 突然倒下；跌倒；倒塌。例如：Be careful not to fall down.
（小心别摔倒。）
5. 寻找。强调寻找的动作。例如：I'm looking for my keys.（我
在找我的钥匙。）
6. 飞向；飞往。例如：He will fly to Shanghai tomorrow.（他明天
将飞往上海。）
7. 处于危险之中。例如：Many animals are in danger.（许多动
物处于危险之中。）
8. 多次；反复地。例如：He read the story over and
over again.（他反复读这个故事。）
9. 感到厌烦。例如：I get bored when I watch the same movie
again.（当我再次看同一部电影时，我感到厌烦。）

（三）阅读理解
未来的生活
In the future, life will be very different. People will live in houses that can
change colors and shapes according to the weather and their mood. These
houses will be very comfortable and energy - saving. They will use solar energy
to heat and cool the rooms, so there will be less pollution.
Transportation will also change a lot. People won't drive cars as often as
they do now. Instead, they will use flying cars or high - speed trains. Flying cars
will be able to take off and land vertically (垂直地), so they won't need long
runways like airplanes. High - speed trains will be much faster than the trains
we have now, and they will be more comfortable.
In the future, people will work at home more often. They will use
computers and the Internet to communicate with their colleagues. Schools will

also be different. Students won't need to go to school every day. They can
study at home through online courses. They will be able to choose the courses

more robots doing jobs, some people may lose their jobs. And if people don't
go out often, they may become less healthy. So, we need to find ways to solve
these problems to make the future life better.
