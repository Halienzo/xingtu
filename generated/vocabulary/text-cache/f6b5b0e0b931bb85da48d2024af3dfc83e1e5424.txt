专题（四）数词 语法综合演练

1.—How was your weekend? 

—Great.It was my grandma’s 　　　　birthday.We got together and enjoyed ourselves. 

A.seventy 　　　　 B.the seventieth 

C.seventieth

2.　　　　 of the students in our class are girls. 

A.Two fifth B.Second fifth C.Two fifths

3.In our hometown, 　　　　villagers leave for big cities to look for jobs. 

A.two hundreds of 　　　　B.hundred of 

C.hundreds of

4.David’s father is 　　　　this year.We just had a special party for his 　　　　birthday last weekend. 

A.fortieth; forty 　　　　B.forty; forty 

C.forty; fortieth 

5.About　　　　 of the students in our class were born in the　　　　. 

A.two thirds; 1990s 　　　　B.two thirds; 1990

C.two third; 1990s 

6.Unit 　　　　is easy but 　　　　unit is difficult. 

A.Sixth; seven 　　　　 B.Six; seven 

C.Six; the seventh 

7.Two days is not enough for him to finish the work.He needs 　　　　day. 

A.other B.a third C.the third

8.Lily came 　　　　in the race. 

A.second B.a second C.the second 

9.—Tom’s mother looks young and beautiful.

—Yes, it’s hard to imagine she is already in her 　　　　. 

A.fifties B.fifty C.fiftieths

10..We can enjoy a 　　　　summer holiday after the Senior High School Entrance Examination. 

A.two months B.two-month C.two-months

11.[河池改编]Please pass me 　　　　book on the right. 

A.the fourth B.four C.fourth

12. There are 　　　　people in Dale’s family. They live on the 　　　　floor. 

A.five; nine B.fifth; nine C.five; ninth

13. We gave away 　　　　books to the school library last year. 

A.six thousands 　　　　B.six thousand 

C.six thousands of

14.　　　　 of the students in our class 　　　　going to the summer camp in Beijing next week. 

A.Two fifths; is 　　　　B.Two fifths; are

C.Second fifths; is

15.—Is Dongjin High-speed Railway Station the 　　　　largest in Hubei Province? 

—Yes, of course. It’s just a little smaller than the largest one in Wuhan.

A.first B.second C.third

【参考答案】

1.C　2.C　3.C　4.C　5.A　6.C　7.B

8.A

9.A　固定搭配:in one’s fifties(在某人50多岁时)。故选A。

10.B　合成词two-month中的month用单数形式。句意:中考后,我们可以享受一个为期2个月的暑假。故选B。

11.A　12.C

13.B　考查数词。thousand与具体数字连用时,表示确数,后面不加-s; 与of连用时,表示概数,词尾要加-s,词前不加数字。故选B。

14.B　考查分数的表达及主谓一致。英语中表达分数时,分子用基数词,分母用序数词,当分子大于一时,分母用序数词的复数形式。这里中心词是复数名词students,作主语时谓语动词用复数形式。故选B。

15.B
