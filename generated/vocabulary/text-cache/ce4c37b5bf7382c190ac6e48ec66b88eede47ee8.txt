一、语法考点
（一）一般过去时
概念：表示过去某个时间发生的动作或存在的状态，常和表示过去的时间状语连用，如
yesterday（昨天）、last week（上周）、two days ago（两天前）等。
构成形式
肯定句：主语 + 动词过去式 + 其他。例如：I visited my grandparents last
weekend.（我上周末看望了我的祖父母。）
否定句：主语 + didn't + 动词原形 + 其他。例如：He didn't go to school
yesterday.（他昨天没去上学。）
一般疑问句：Did + 主语 + 动词原形 + 其他？肯定回答：Yes, 主语 + did. 否
定回答：No, 主语 + didn't. 例如：Did you go to the park yesterday? Yes, I did.
/ No, I didn't.（你昨天去公园了吗？是的，我去了。/ 不，我没去。）
动词过去式变化规则
规则变化
一般在动词原形末尾加 -ed，如：look - looked，play - played。
以不发音的字母 e 结尾的动词，直接加 -d，如：live - lived，hope -
hoped。
末尾只有一个辅音字母的重读闭音节词，先双写这个辅音字母，再加 -ed，
如：stop - stopped，plan - planned。
以 “辅音字母 + y” 结尾的动词，先变 y 为 i，再加 -ed，如：study -
studied，carry - carried。
不规则变化：需特殊记忆，如：go - went，do - did，see - saw，have - had 等。

（二）不定代词
概念：不指明代替任何特定名词或形容词的代词。
常见不定代词：something（某事；某物）、anything（任何事；任何物）、nothing
（没有什么）、someone（某人）、anyone（任何人）、no one（没有人）、everyone
（每个人）等。
用法
something 常用于肯定句中；anything 常用于否定句和疑问句中，但在表示请
求、建议，希望得到肯定回答的疑问句中用 something。
例如：I have something to tell you.（我有某事要告诉你。）
Do you have anything to say?（你有什么要说的吗？）
Would you like something to eat?（你想吃点什么吗？）
nothing 表示否定意义，相当于 “not...anything”。
例如：I saw nothing. = I didn't see anything.（我什么也没看见。）
someone、anyone、no one、everyone 等指人，它们的用法与 something、
anything 等类似。
例如：Someone is knocking at the door.（有人在敲门。）
Is there anyone in the classroom?（教室里有人吗？）
二、词汇考点
（一）重点单词
1. pron. 任何人，常用于否定句和疑问句中。例如：Did you meet anyone
interesting?（你遇到有趣的人了吗？）
2. adj. 精彩的；绝妙的，可用来描述事物或经历。例如：We had a
wonderful time at the party.（我们在聚会上玩得很开心。）
3. adj. & pron. 不多；很少，修饰可数名词复数，表示否定意义；a few 表示 “一
些；几个”，表示肯定意义 。例如：Few people like snakes.（很少有人喜欢蛇。）
I have a few friends here.（我在这里有几个朋友。）

4. 相当多；不少，修饰可数名词复数。例如：He has quite a few books.
（他有相当多的书。）
5. adj., adv. & pron. 最多；大多数；大部分，可以修饰名词、动词等。例如：
Most students like English.（大多数学生喜欢英语。）I like math most.（我最喜欢
数学。）
6. pron. 某事；某物，常用于肯定句。例如：There is something wrong
with my bike.（我的自行车出毛病了。）
7. pron. 没有什么；没有一件东西，表否定意义。例如：I have nothing to
do today.（我今天无事可做。）
8. pron. 每人；人人；所有人，强调整体。例如：Everyone is here today.
（今天每个人都在这儿。）
9. pron. 我自己，反身代词。例如：I can do it myself.（我能自己做这件事。）
10. pron. 你自己，反身代词，复数形式为 yourselves（你们自己）。例如：
Help yourself to some fruit.（请随便吃些水果。）
（二）重点短语
1. 去度假，等同于 “take a vacation”“have a vacation”。例如：Where
did you go on vacation?（你去哪里度假了？）
2. 待在家里。例如：I often stay at home on weekends.（我周末经常
待在家里。）
3. 去爬山。例如：We went to the mountains last Sunday.（我
们上周日去爬山了。）
4. 去海滩。例如：They like going to the beach in summer.（他们
夏天喜欢去海滩。）
5. 参观博物馆。例如：We visited some museums during the trip.
（我们在旅行中参观了一些博物馆。）
6. 相当多；不少，修饰可数名词复数。例如：He took quite a few photos.
（他拍了相当多的照片。）
7. 大部分时间。例如：I stayed at home and read books most of the
time.（我大部分时间都待在家里看书。）
8. 尝起来不错，“taste” 是系动词，后接形容词作表语。类似的系动词还
有 look（看起来）、sound（听起来）、feel（感觉起来）等。例如：The food tastes
good.（这食物尝起来不错。）
9. 玩得开心，等同于 “enjoy oneself”“have fun”。例如：We had
a good time at the party.（我们在聚会上玩得很开心。）

三、同步练习
（一）单项选择
1. — Did you go ________ interesting on vacation?
— Yes, I went to Hangzhou.
A. somewhere B. anywhere C. nowhere D. everywhere
 答案：B
 解析：anywhere 常用于否定句和疑问句，意为 “任何地方” ；somewhere 用于肯
定句；nowhere 表示 “无处”；everywhere 表示 “到处”。本句是疑问句，询问假期
是否去了有趣的地方，所以用 anywhere，选 B。
2. I didn't buy anything for ________. I think they're too expensive.
A. myself B. yourself C. himself D. themselves
 答案：A
 解析：句子主语是 I，反身代词要用 myself，表示 “为我自己”。句意为 “我没给我
自己买任何东西，我觉得它们太贵了”，所以选 A。
3. — How was your vacation, Lily?
— ________. I had a great time.
A. Terrible B. Boring C. Wonderful D. Not good
 答案：C
 解析：根据回答 “I had a great time.”（我玩得很开心）可知假期很棒。Terrible 意
为 “糟糕的”；Boring 意为 “无聊的”；Wonderful 意为 “精彩的”；Not good 意为
“不好的”。所以选 C。
4. There are ________ people in the supermarket. It's so crowded.
A. few B. a few C. quite a few D. little
 答案：C

 解析：few 表示 “很少，几乎没有”，修饰可数名词复数，表否定；a few 表示 “一
些”，修饰可数名词复数；quite a few 意为 “相当多”，修饰可数名词复数；little 修
饰不可数名词，表示 “很少”。根据 “It's so crowded.”（太拥挤了）可知人很多，所
以选 C。
5. — Did you ________ any photos in the mountains?
— Yes, I did.
A. take B. took C. taking D. takes
 答案：A
 解析：在一般过去时的一般疑问句中，did 后面要用动词原形。本句问 “你在山里
拍照了吗”，所以用 take，选 A。
6. — Did you meet ________ famous on your trip?
— No, I didn't.
A. someone B. anyone C. everyone D. no one
 答案：B
 解析：anyone 用于否定句和疑问句中，意为 “任何人”；someone 用于肯定句；
everyone 意为 “每个人”；no one 意为 “没有人”。本句是疑问句，询问 “你在旅
行中遇到名人了吗”，所以用 anyone，选 B。
7. The food in that restaurant ________ delicious. I like it very much.
A. looks B. sounds C. tastes D. feels
 答案：C
 解析：looks 意为 “看起来”；sounds 意为 “听起来”；tastes 意为 “尝起来”；feels
意为 “感觉起来”。因为主语是 “The food”（食物），所以是 “尝起来美味”，选 C。
8. — Where did you go last weekend?
— I ________ at home and watched TV.
A. stay B. stayed C. stays D. am staying
 答案：B
 解析：根据 “last weekend”（上周末）可知句子时态是一般过去时，stay 的过去式
是 stayed，所以选 B。
9. — How many students are there in the classroom?
— ________. They are all on the playground.
A. No one B. None C. Someone D. Anyone

 答案：B

Anyone 意为 “任何人” 。本句是回答数量，所以选 B。

 答案：B
 解析：anything 用于否定句和疑问句中；something 用于肯定句；nothing 本身

12. — Did you go to the park with ________?

interesting 修饰物，意为 “有趣的”；interested 修饰人，意为 “感兴趣的”。本句

（三）阅读理解
Last summer vacation, I went to Beijing with my family. We visited many
interesting places. On the first day, we went to Tian'anmen Square. It was really
large and there were many people there. We took some photos. Then we went to
the Palace Museum. I learned a lot about Chinese history there. On the second
day, we visited the Great Wall. It was very long and wonderful. We walked on the
Great Wall for a long time. We were tired but happy. On the third day, we went
shopping. I bought some gifts for my friends. We had a great time in Beijing.
1. ( )Where did the writer go last summer vacation?
A. Shanghai. B. Beijing. C. Hangzhou. D. Guangzhou.
答案：B。解析：根据 “Last summer vacation, I went to Beijing with my family.” 可
知作者去了北京。
2. ( )What did they do on the first day?
A. They visited the Great Wall.
B. They went shopping.
C. They visited Tian'anmen Square and the Palace Museum.
D. They took a walk.
答案：C。解析：根据 “On the first day, we went to Tian'an men Square...Then we
went to the Palace Museum.” 可知第一天他们去了天安门广场和故宫博物院。
3. ( )How did they feel after walking on the Great Wall?
A. Tired and sad. B. Tired but happy. C. Relaxed. D. Bored.
答案：B。解析：根据 “We walked on the Great Wall for a long time. We were tired
but happy.” 可知他们很累但很开心。
4. ( )What did the writer do on the third day?
A. Bought some gifts. B. Took some photos.
