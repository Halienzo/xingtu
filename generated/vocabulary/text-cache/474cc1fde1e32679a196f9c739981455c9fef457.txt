Unit 8 Making a Difference 单元重点知识

一、词汇巩固

（一）核心名词（志愿服务 & 帮助行为相关）

1. volunteer /ˌvɒlənˈtɪə(r)/ (n.) 志愿者；(v.) 自愿做

例：Robert wants to be a volunteer at the animal shelter to help take care of sick dogs.（罗伯特想成为动物收容所的志愿者，帮忙照顾生病的狗狗。）

2. organization /ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃn/ (n.) 组织；机构

例：Blue Sky Rescue is a well-known organization that provides aid during natural disasters.（蓝天救援队是知名组织，在自然灾害期间提供援助。）

3. donation /dəʊˈneɪʃn/ (n.) 捐赠物；捐赠

例：Rose collects donations of food and toys for the animals at the shelter every month.（罗斯每月为收容所的动物收集食物和玩具捐赠。）

4. aid /eɪd/ (n./v.) 帮助；援助

例：The volunteer team provided medical aid to the victims of the typhoon.（志愿团队为台风受害者提供了医疗援助。）

5. shelter /ˈʃeltə(r)/ (n.) 庇护所；收容所

例：The animal shelter takes in lost or homeless pets and tries to find them new owners. （动物收容所接收走失或无家可归的宠物，并努力为它们寻找新主人。）

6. teamwork /ˈtiːmwɜːk/ (n.) 团队合作

例：Successful rescue missions depend on good teamwork—every volunteer has an

important role.（成功的救援任务依靠良好的团队合作，每个志愿者都发挥重要作用。）

（二）重点动词（志愿行为 & 帮助动作）

1. provide /prəˈvaɪd/ (v.) 提供；给予

例：The community center provides free meals for the elderly every weekend.（社区中心每周为老人提供免费餐食。）

2. donate /dəʊˈneɪt/ (v.) 捐赠；赠送

例：Harry donates his pocket money to the charity organization to help people in need.（哈利把零花钱捐给慈善组织，帮助有需要的人。）

3. rescue /ˈreskjuː/ (v./n.) 营救；救援

例：Blue Sky Rescue volunteers rescued many people trapped in the earthquake.（蓝天救援队志愿者营救了许多被困在地震中的人。）

4. guide /ɡaɪd/ (v.) 引导；指引；(n.) 向导

例：Volunteers guide visitors through the museum and explain the history of the paintings. （志愿者引导游客参观博物馆，并讲解画作的历史。）

5. sign up 报名；注册

例：Ella signed up with the youth group to volunteer at the nursing home.（埃拉报名加入青年团体，去养老院做志愿者。）

6. clean up 清理；打扫

例：Volunteers clean up the beach every month to keep it free from litter.（志愿者每月清理海滩，保持海滩无垃圾。）

（三）关键形容词（志愿精神 & 帮助特征）

1. voluntary /ˈvɒləntri/ (adj.) 自愿的；无偿的

例：Voluntary work means you don’t get paid, but it brings a lot of personal satisfaction. （志愿工作是无偿的，但能带来巨大的个人满足感。）

2. willing /ˈwɪlɪŋ/ (adj.) 愿意的；乐意的

例：A good volunteer should be willing to help others even in difficult situations.（优秀的志愿者应愿意在困难情况下仍帮助他人。）

3. helpful /ˈhelpfl/ (adj.) 有帮助的；有益的

例：Teaching the elderly to use mobile phones is helpful—it makes their lives easier.（教老人用手机很有帮助，能让他们的生活更便捷。）

4. ordinary /ˈɔːdnri/ (adj.) 平凡的；普通的

例：Blue Sky Rescue volunteers are ordinary people, but they do extraordinary things to save lives.（蓝天救援队志愿者是普通人，但他们做着不平凡的救人事迹。）

5. active /ˈæktɪv/ (adj.) 活跃的；积极的

例：Teng Fei is active in volunteer work—he visits the nursing home every weekend.（腾飞积极参与志愿工作，每周都去养老院。）

二、词形变换

1. help (v./n. 帮助) → helpful (adj. 有帮助的) → helpless (adj. 无助的) → helpfully (adv.

有帮助地)

例：Volunteers help the elderly with their shopping every week.（志愿者每周帮老人购物。）

Teaching the elderly to use smartphones is helpful for their daily lives.（教老人用智能手机对他们的日常生活很有帮助。）

The lost child felt helpless until a volunteer found him.（走失的孩子感到无助，直到志愿者找到他。）

The volunteer explained the rules helpfully, so everyone understood quickly.（志愿者耐心地解释规则，大家很快就明白了。）

2. volunteer (n. 志愿者 /v. 自愿) → voluntary (adj. 自愿的) → voluntarily (adv. 自愿地)

例：Rose has been a volunteer at the animal shelter for two years.（罗斯在动物收容所做志愿者两年了。）

Many people take part in voluntary work during their free time.（很多人在空闲时间参与志愿工作。）

He voluntarily gave up his weekend to clean up the park.（他自愿放弃周末，去清理公园。）

3. success (n. 成功) → successful (adj. 成功的) → successfully (adv. 成功地)

例：The rescue mission was a great success—all trapped people were saved.（救援任务大获成功，所有被困人员都获救了。）

Blue Sky Rescue has made many successful rescues during natural disasters.（蓝天救援队在自然灾害期间完成了多次成功救援。）

The volunteer team successfully organized a charity event to collect donations.（志愿团队成功组织了一场慈善活动筹集捐赠。）

4. donate (v. 捐赠) → donation (n. 捐赠物 / 捐赠) → donor (n. 捐赠者)

例：People often donate clothes and books to children in poor areas.（人们常向贫困地区的孩子捐赠衣物和书籍。）

The organization received a large donation of food from a local supermarket.（组织收到当地超市捐赠的大量食物。）

The donor asked to remain anonymous because he didn’t want attention.（捐赠者要求匿名，因为他不想引人注目。）

5. active (adj. 活跃的) → activity (n. 活动) → actively (adv. 积极地)

例：Teng Fei is an active volunteer who takes part in many events.（腾飞是活跃的志愿者，参与很多活动。）

The community center holds volunteer activities every month, like cleaning up parks.（社区中心每月举办志愿活动，如清理公园。）

She actively helps organize the events and encourages others to join.（她积极帮忙组织活动，鼓励其他人参与。）

三、重点短语

1. make a difference 有（重要）作用；产生影响

例：Even small acts of kindness, like helping an old person cross the road, can make a difference.（即使是帮老人过马路这样的小事，也能产生重要影响。）

2. sign up 报名；注册

例：If you want to volunteer at the animal shelter, you need to sign up first and attend a short training.（如果想在动物收容所做志愿者，你需要先报名并参加简短培训。）

3. clean up 清理；打扫

例：Every weekend, volunteers go to the beach to clean up litter and protect the environment.（每周周末，志愿者去海滩清理垃圾，保护环境。）

4. provide aid 提供援助

例：During the typhoon, Blue Sky Rescue provided aid to people who lost their homes.（台风期间，蓝天救援队为失去家园的人提供援助。）

5. in need 处于贫困中；需要帮助

例：The charity organization helps people in need by providing them with food and warm clothes.（慈善组织通过提供食物和保暖衣物，帮助有需要的人。）

6. help out 分担工作；帮忙做

例：When the animal shelter is busy, volunteers come to help out with feeding and cleaning.（动物收容所忙碌时，志愿者会来帮忙喂食和打扫。）

7. hand out 分发；散发

例：At the community party, volunteers hand out free books to children from poor families. （社区派对上，志愿者向贫困家庭的孩子分发免费书籍。）

8. ever since 自从； 自 … … 以后

例：He has been a volunteer at the nursing home ever since he retired.（他退休后就一直在养老院做志愿者。）

9. take care of 照顾；照料

例：Volunteers take care of sick animals at the shelter until they are healthy enough to be adopted.（志愿者在收容所照顾生病的动物，直到它们健康到可以被领养。）

四、重点句型

1. 谈论志愿经历（现在完成时 + since/for）：I have been a volunteer + 地点 / 组织 + for + 时间段 /since + 过去时间点.

例：I have been a volunteer at the animal shelter for two years.（我在动物收容所做志愿者两年了。）Rose has been a volunteer with the youth group since last summer.（罗斯自从去年夏天就一直是青年团体的志愿者。）

2. 描述志愿工作内容：As a volunteer, I + 具体动作（e.g., clean up /provide aid /guide visitors）+ to help + 帮助对象.

例：As a volunteer at the museum, I guide visitors and provide information about the

exhibits to help them learn more.（作为博物馆志愿者，我引导游客并讲解展品信息，

帮他们了解更多内容。）As a volunteer at the nursing home, Teng Fei exercises with the elderly to help them stay active.（作为养老院志愿者，腾飞陪老人锻炼，帮他们保持活跃。）

3. 表达志愿工作的意义：Doing volunteer work makes me + 感受（e.g., happy /satisfied） because + 原因（e.g., I can help others /learn new skills）.

例：Doing volunteer work makes me happy because I can help animals in need.（做志愿工作让我开心，因为我能帮助有需要的动物。）Doing volunteer work makes me satisfied because I can use my skills to make a difference.（做志愿工作让我满足，因为我能运用自己的技能产生影响。）

4. 邀请他人参与志愿活动：Would you like to + 志愿动作（e.g., join us /be a volunteer）

+ at + 地点？It’s a great way to + 志愿意义（e.g., help others /meet new people）.

例：Would you like to join us as a volunteer at the beach clean-up? It’s a great way to

protect the environment.（你愿意加入我们，成为海滩清理的志愿者吗？这是保护环境的好方式。）Would you like to be a volunteer at the animal shelter? It’s a great way to care

for homeless pets.（你愿意成为动物收容所的志愿者吗？这是照顾无家可归宠物的好方式。）

5. 说明志愿工作的困难与收获：Volunteer work is + 困难（e.g., tiring /challenging）, but + 收获（e.g., it teaches me.../it brings me... ）.

例：Volunteer work at the shelter is tiring, but it teaches me the importance of patience.（收容所的志愿工作很累，但它教会我耐心的重要性。）Volunteer work during disasters is challenging, but it brings me a strong sense of achievement.（灾害期间的志愿工作很有挑战性，但它给我带来强烈的成就感。）

6. 介绍志愿组织 的功 能 ： [Organization] + 核 心动作（ e.g., provides aid /takes in

/organizes activities）+ to help + 帮助对象 + 达成目标（e.g., stay safe /find homes /improve lives）.

例：Blue Sky Rescue provides medical aid and searches for missing people to help victims of natural disasters stay safe.（蓝天救援队提供医疗援助并搜救失踪人员，帮助自然灾害受害者保障安全。）The animal shelter takes in homeless pets and organizes adoption events to help them find new homes.（动物收容所接收无家可归的宠物并组织领养活动，帮它们找到新家。）

7. 用 “not only...but also...” 描述志愿工作的多重收获：Volunteering not only helps +帮助对象，but also helps volunteers + 个人收获（e.g., learn skills /make friends）.

例：Volunteering at the nursing home not only helps the elderly feel less lonely, but also helps volunteers learn about life experiences from the old generation1 .Volunteering with

Blue Sky Rescue not only saves people’s lives, but also helps volunteers develop

teamwork skills5 .（该句型呼应文档中 “志愿工作双赢” 的理念，结合养老院服务、蓝天救援队救援等具体场景，体现 “帮助他人” 与 “自我成长” 的双重价值。）

8. 用 “even though” 描述志愿工作中的让步关系：Even though volunteer work is + 困

难（e.g., hard /unpaid）, it’s still + 积极评价（e.g., worth it /meaningful）because + 原因.

例：Even though volunteer work at the animal shelter is tiring, it’s still worth it because we can help homeless pets find new homes17 .Even though Blue Sky Rescue’s work is

dangerous, it’s still meaningful because every rescue can save a life6 .（该句型贴合文档中蓝天救援队志愿者 “虽面临困难仍坚持” 的表述，如 “工作无偿、无眠无食但仍坚持救援” ，体现志愿精神的坚定 6 。）

9. 询问他人志愿经历（现在完成时 + 疑问词）：How long have you been volunteering + 地点？/ What do you usually do when you volunteer + 地点？

例：How long have you been volunteering at the museum? — I’ve been volunteering here for two weeks.What do you usually do when you volunteer at the beach? — I usually pick up litter and clean the shore.（该句型直接对应文档中 Section A 的听力采访场景，如对足球组织、博物馆、海滩清理组织志愿者的提问，符合 “交流志愿经历” 的单元任务。）

10. 描述志愿活动的组织与安排：The volunteer activity + 时间（e.g., takes place every

Saturday /will be held next month）+ 地点，and volunteers need to + 准备工作（e.g., bring tools /attend training）.

例：The beach clean-up activity takes place every Saturday morning at Sunshine Park, and volunteers need to bring gloves and garbage bag 、The nursing home visit will be held next Sunday, and volunteers need to attend a short training to learn how to communicate with

the elderly.（该句型贴合文档中 “志愿活动策划” 的内容，如社区志愿活动的时间、地点及准备要求，符合 Project 环节 “组建志愿小组” 的任务需求。）

五、重点语法

（一）现在完成时（Present Perfect Tense）与 since/for 的连用（单元

核心语法）

1. 核心用法

表示从过去持续到现在的志愿经历：for 后接 “时间段”（如 two years, a few months）， since 后接 “过去时间点”（如 last summer, 2022），是单元描述 “志愿时长” 的核心语法。

例：Rose has been a volunteer at the animal shelter for two years18 .Volunteer 3 has been a beach clean-up volunteer since 2017（根据 “for about seven years” 推导）

与 already/yet/never/ever 的搭配：

. already（已完成，肯定句）：I have already signed up for the nursing home volunteer work2.

. yet（未完成，否定句 / 疑问句）：Have you finished the volunteer training yet?

. never（从未，否定句）：He has never volunteered at an animal shelter before19 .

. ever（曾经，疑问句）：Have you ever taken part in a disaster rescue volunteer activity?

2. 句式结构与易错点

句式类型

结构

文档例句

肯定句

主语 + have/has + 过去

分词 + for/since...

Teng Fei has volunteered at the nursing home since last year.

否定句

主语 + haven’t/hasn’t +过去分词 + for/since...

Robert hasn’t volunteered at an animal shelter yet.

疑问句

Have/Has + 主语 + 过去

分词 + for/since...?

How long has Volunteer 2 been a guide at the museum?

. 易错点提醒：非延续性动词（如 join, start, finish）不能与 for/since 连用，需转化为延续性动词（如 join→be a member of, start→be）。

例：错误：He has joined the volunteer group for a year.正确：He has been a member of the volunteer group for a year.

（二）连词 even though/although（让步状语从句）

1. 核心用法

. 表示 “志愿工作中的困难与坚持”：引导让步从句，说明 “尽管存在困难，仍坚持志愿行为” ，呼应文档中蓝天救援队的精神表述。

例：Even though the volunteer work is unpaid, many people still take part in it because

they want to help others6 .Although the beach clean-up is tiring, Volunteer 3 has kept doing it for seven years.

2. 用法注意

. 不能与 but 同时使用：even though/although 本身表 “转折” ，若再用 but 会造成语法冗余。

例：错误：Although the work is hard, but it’s meaningful.正确：Although the work is hard, it’s meaningful.

（三）动词不定式（Infinitives）作目的状语

1. 核心用法

. 表示 “志愿动作的目的”：说明 “做志愿工作是为了达成某一目标”，是单元描述 “志愿意义” 的重要语法。

例：Volunteers go to the nursing home to help the elderly exercise（ 目的：帮助老人锻炼） Blue Sky Rescue teaches people safety skills to help them prepare for natural disasters（ 目的：帮助人们应对灾害）

2. 句式变换

. 否定式：not to + 动词原形，表 “志愿行为需避免的事”。

例：Volunteers need to remember not to talk loudly when visiting the nursing home（避免大声喧哗）

（四）并列连词 not only...but also...（单元拓展语法）

1. 核心用法

. 表示 “志愿工作的双重影响”：连接 “帮助他人” 与 “自我成长”，体现 “志愿行为的双向价值” ，贴合文档中 “帮助他人即帮助自己” 的理念。

例：Volunteering not only helps homeless pets find homes, but also helps volunteers learn to be responsible17 .Blue Sky Rescue’s work not only saves lives, but also teaches

volunteers practical life-saving skills.

2. 用法注意

. 主谓一致：连接两个主语时，谓语动词与 “but also” 后的主语保持一致。

例：Not only the students but also the teacher is going to volunteer at the animal shelter.
