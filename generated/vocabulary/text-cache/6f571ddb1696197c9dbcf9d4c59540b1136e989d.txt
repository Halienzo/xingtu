Unit 7 A Good Read 单元重点知识

一、词汇巩固

（一）核心名词（书籍类型 & 文学相关）

1. fiction /ˈfɪkʃn/ (n.) 小说

例：Science fiction is my favorite type of fiction—I love books about space and aliens.（科幻小说是我最喜欢的小说类型 —— 我喜欢关于太空和外星人的书。）

2. adventure /ədˈventʃə(r)/ (n.) 冒险；冒险经历

例：Around the World in Eighty Days is full of exciting adventures in different countries. （《八十天环游世界》里充满了在不同国家的刺激冒险经历。）

3. detective /dɪˈtektɪv/ (n.) 侦探

例：Sherlock Holmes, the famous detective in The Final Problem, is good at solving

mysterious cases.（《最后一案》中著名的侦探夏洛克・福尔摩斯擅长破解悬疑案件。）

4. summary /ˈsʌməri/ (n.) 总结；概要

例：Before writing the book report, we need to write a summary of the story to list the main events.（写读书报告前，我们需要写故事概要，列出主要情节。）

5. idiom /ˈɪdiəm/ (n.) 习语；成语

例：Reading The Romance of the Three Kingdoms helps us learn a lot of Chinese idioms, like “three cobblers with their wits combined equal Zhuge Liang the mastermind” .（读《三国演义》能帮我们学到很多中国成语，比如 “三个臭皮匠，顶个诸葛亮” 。）

6. recommendation /ˌrekəmenˈdeɪʃn/ (n.) 推荐；提议

例：Can you give me a recommendation for a good fantasy novel? I want to read one during the holiday.（你能给我推荐一本好的奇幻小说吗？我想假期读一本。）

（二）重点动词（阅读 & 书籍相关动作）

1. recommend /ˌrekəˈmend/ (v.) 推荐；建议

例：My teacher recommended The Secret Garden to me—it’s a warm story about

friendship and growth.（老师向我推荐了《秘密花园》—— 这是一个关于友谊和成长的温暖故事。）

2. publish /ˈpʌblɪʃ/ (v.) 出版；发表

例：Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland was published in 1865 and has been popular ever since.（《爱丽丝梦游仙境》于 1865 年出版，从那以后一直很受欢迎。）

3. translate /trænsˈleɪt/ (v.) 翻译

例：Many Chinese classic novels, like Journey to the West, have been translated into

different languages for readers around the world.（很多中国经典小说，如《西游记》，已被翻译成不同语言，供全球读者阅读。）

4. solve /sɒlv/ (v.) 解决；解答

例：Sherlock Holmes can solve even the most difficult cases with his sharp observation. （夏洛克・福尔摩斯凭借敏锐的观察力，甚至能破解最难的案件。）

5. adapt /əˈdæpt/ (v.) 改编；适应

例：Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone was adapted into a popular movie in 2001. （《哈利・波特与魔法石》在 2001 年被改编成一部受欢迎的电影。）

6. reflect /rɪˈflekt/ (v.) 反映；体现

例：Oliver Twist reflects the hard life of poor people in London in the 1800s.（《雾都孤儿》反映了 19 世纪伦敦穷人的艰难生活。）

（三）关键形容词（书籍特征 & 阅读感受）

1. classic /ˈklæsɪk/ (adj.) 经典的；古典的 (n.) 经典作品

例：The Old Man and the Sea is a classic novel that teaches us never to give up.（《老人与海》是一部经典小说，教会我们永不放弃。）

2. mysterious /mɪˈstɪəriəs/ (adj.) 神秘的；难以理解的

例：The plot of The Final Problem is so mysterious that I couldn’t put the book down until

I finished it.（《最后一案》的情节太神秘了，我一口气读到结尾才放下书。）

3. exciting /ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋ/ (adj.) 令人兴奋的；使人激动的

例：The adventure stories in Around the World in Eighty Days are so exciting that they

make me want to travel around the world.（《八十天环游世界》里的冒险故事太令人兴奋了，让我想去环游世界。）

4. emotional /ɪˈməʊʃənl/ (adj.) 情感的；情绪的

例：The Little Match Girl is an emotional story—it always makes me cry when I read it. （《卖火柴的小女孩》是一个感人的故事 —— 我读的时候总会哭。）

5. thought-provoking /Iθɔ:t prəvəʊkɪŋ/ (adj.) 引人深思的

例：The Three-Body Problem is a thought-provoking science fiction novel that makes us think about the future of humans and aliens.（《三体》是一部引人深思的科幻小说，让我们思考人类与外星人的未来。）

二、词形变换

1. mystery (n. 神秘；悬疑小说) → mysterious (adj. 神秘的) → mysteriously (adv. 神秘地)

例：The mystery of the locked garden in The Secret Garden attracts many readers.（《秘密花园》中上锁花园的谜团吸引了很多读者。）

The old key found by Mary has a mysterious power to unlock the secret garden.（玛丽找到的旧钥匙有打开秘密花园的神秘力量。）

The elf disappeared mysteriously after turning Nils into a small creature.（小精灵把尼尔斯变成小动物后，神秘地消失了。）

2. excite (v. 使 兴 奋) → exciting (adj. 令 人 兴 奋 的) → excited (adj. 感 到 兴 奋 的) → excitement (n. 兴奋)

例：Adventure stories always excite me because they are full of unexpected events.（冒险故事总能让我兴奋，因为它们充满意外情节。）

Around the World in Eighty Days is an exciting book about a man’s journey around the globe.（《八十天环游世界》是一本关于环球旅行的令人兴奋的书。）

I was excited when I finished reading Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone—I

couldn’t wait to read the next book.（读完《哈利・波特与魔法石》时我很兴奋，迫不及待想读下一本。）

The excitement of solving the mystery in The Final Problem made me stay up late reading. （破解《最后一案》中谜团的兴奋感，让我熬夜读完了书。）

3. reflect (v. 反映；思考) → reflective (adj. 沉思的；反映情况的) → reflection (n. 反映；反思)

例：Good books reflect the real life of different times and cultures.（好书反映不同时代和文化的真实生活。）

After reading The Old Man and the Sea, I became reflective about the meaning of “never giving up” .（读完《老人与海》后，我开始思考 “永不放弃” 的意义。）

The novel is a reflection of the social problems in 19th-century London.（这部小说是 19世纪伦敦社会问题的反映。）

4. recommend (v. 推荐) → recommendation (n. 推荐) → recommendable (adj. 值得推荐的)

例：My friend recommended The Secret Garden to me, and I found it really interesting.（朋友向我推荐了《秘密花园》，我发现这本书确实很有趣。）Can you give me a

recommendation for a good science fiction novel?（你能给我推荐一本好的科幻小说吗？）The Three-Body Problem is highly recommendable for readers who love science and philosophy.（《三体》非常值得推荐给喜欢科学和哲学的读者。）

5. emotion (n. 情感) → emotional (adj. 情感的；感人的) → emotionally (adv. 在情感上)

例：Classic novels often arouse strong emotions in readers, like sadness or joy.（经典小说常唤起读者强烈的情感，如悲伤或快乐。）The Little Match Girl is an emotional story that touches the hearts of many readers.（《卖火柴的小女孩》是一个感人的故事，触动了很多读者的心。）Reading My Childhood made me emotionally connected to the main character’s struggles.（读《童年》让我在情感上与主角的困境产生了共鸣。）

三、重点短语

1. book report 读书报告

例：The teacher asked us to write a book report about a classic novel we have read recently. （老师让我们写一篇读书报告，介绍最近读的一本经典小说。）

2. science fiction 科幻小说

例：The Three-Body Problem is one of the most famous science fiction novels in China. （《三体》是中国最著名的科幻小说之一。）

3. put down 放下（书等）

例：The plot of The Final Problem is so interesting that I couldn’t put it down until I finished reading it.（《最后一案》的情节太有趣了，我一口气读完才放下书。）

4. hear of 听说；得知

例：Have you ever heard of The Wonderful Wizard of Oz? It’s a classic fantasy novel.（你听说过《绿野仙踪》吗？它是一部经典的奇幻小说。）

5. based on 以 … … 为基础；根据

例：The movie Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone is based on the novel of the same name by J.K. Rowling.（电影《哈利・波特与魔法石》根据 J.K. 罗琳的同名小说改编。）

6. in one’s opinion 在某人看来

例：In my opinion, The Old Man and the Sea is a must-read because it teaches us to be

strong in the face of difficulties.（在我看来，《老人与海》是必读书籍，因为它教会我们在困难面前要坚强。）

7. learn from 从 … … 中学到

例：We can learn a lot of life lessons from great books, like honesty and courage.（我们能从好书中学习很多人生道理，如诚实和勇气。）

8. take place 发生

例：Most events in The Romance of the Three Kingdoms take place in ancient China

during the Three Kingdoms period.（《三国演义》中的大部分事件发生在古代中国的三国时期。）

9. stay up late 熬夜

例：I stayed up late reading Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland because I wanted to know what happened to Alice next.（我熬夜读《爱丽丝梦游仙境》，因为想知道爱丽丝接下来会发生什么。）

四、重点句型

1. 询问是否读过某本书（现在完成时）：Have you ever read + 书名？/ Have you heard of + 书名？

例：Have you ever read The Secret Garden? It’s a warm story about friendship.（你读过《秘密花园》吗？这是一个关于友谊的温暖故事。）Have you heard of Around the World in Eighty Days? It’s full of exciting adventures.（你听说过《八十天环游世界》吗？里面充满了刺激的冒险。）

2. 描述阅读感受与收获：Reading + 书名 + makes me + 感受（adj.）/teaches me to + 动作.

例：Reading The Old Man and the Sea makes me feel brave and teaches me never to give up.（读《老人与海》让我感到勇敢，也教会我永不放弃。）Reading Oliver Twist teaches me to be kind to others, even when life is hard.（读《雾都孤儿》教会我即使生活艰难，也要对他人友善。）

3. 介绍书籍的基本信息（作者、类型、主要内容）：书名 + is written by + 作者. It’s a + 书籍类型 + about + 主要内容.

例：Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland is written by Lewis Carroll. It’s a fantasy novel about a girl who enters a magical world.（《爱丽丝梦游仙境》由刘易斯・卡罗尔所著，是一部关于女孩进入奇幻世界的奇幻小说。）The Final Problem is written by Arthur Conan Doyle. It’s a mystery novel about the detective Sherlock Holmes.（《最后一案》由阿瑟・柯南・道尔所著，是一部关于侦探夏洛克・福尔摩斯的悬疑小说。）

4. 对比书籍与影视：I prefer the book to the movie because + 原因. / The movie is based on the book, but + 差异.

例：I prefer the book Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone to the movie because the

book has more detailed descriptions.（比起电影，我更喜欢《哈利・波特与魔法石》这本书，因为书中有更详细的描写。）The movie The Secret Garden is based on the book, but it cuts some small events in the original story.（电影《秘密花园》根据书籍改编，但删减了原著中的一些小情节。）

5. 推荐书籍并说明理由：I recommend + 书名 + to you because + 理由. It’s + 书籍特征（adj.）, and you will + 阅读收获.

例：I recommend The Little Prince to you because it’s full of wisdom. It’s

thought-provoking, and you will learn about love and friendship.（我向你推荐《小王子》，因为它充满智慧。这本书引人深思，你会学到关于爱与友谊的道理。）I

recommend Journey to the West to you because it’s a Chinese classic. It’s exciting, and you will learn a lot of Chinese culture.（我向你推荐《西游记》，因为它是中国经典。这本书很精彩，你会学到很多中国文化。）

6. 描述书籍中的核心情节：In the story, + 主角 + 主要动作 + because + 原因. Finally, + 结局.

例：In the story, Mary finds a secret garden because she hears about it from a local. Finally, she works with Dickon to make the garden beautiful again.（在故事中，玛丽因为从当地人那里听说了秘密花园而找到它。最后，她和迪康一起让花园重新变得美丽。）In the story, the old fisherman fights a huge fish for days because he wants to prove his strength. Finally, sharks eat the fish, but he shows that he is not defeated.（在故事中，老渔夫和一条大鱼搏斗了几天，因为他想证明自己的力量。最后，鲨鱼吃掉了鱼，但他证明了自己没有被打败。）

7. 用现在完成时描述阅读经历（already/yet/never/ever）：I have already read + 书名. /

I haven’t read + 书名 + yet. / Have you ever read + 书名？

例：I have already read The Romance of the Three Kingdoms twice—it helps me

understand many Chinese idioms.I haven’t read The Little Prince yet, but I’ve already

borrowed it from the library.Have you ever read Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland? Fu

Xing says it’s a wonderful fantasy novel.（该句型直接对应文档中 Grammar Focus 对现

在完成时的应用要求，结合 already/yet/never/ever 等标志词，贴合 Section A 对话中“交流阅读进度” 的场景，如 Peter 与 Fu Xing 讨论书籍阅读情况。）

8. 用 “so...that” 描述书籍的吸引力（情节 / 感受）：The + 书籍特征（e.g., plot/story） of + 书名 + is so + 形容词（e.g., interesting/exciting）that + 阅读结果.

例：The plot of The Final Problem is so mysterious that I couldn’t put the book down until midnight18 .The story of The Secret Garden is so warm that it makes me believe in the

power of friendship.（该句型呼应文档中 “so...that 引导结果状语从句” 的语法点，结合《最后一案》的悬疑情节、《秘密花园》的温暖主题等具体案例，体现书籍对读者的强烈吸引力。）

9. 说明书籍与现实的关联（反映社会 / 传递道理）：书名 + reflects + 现实内容（e.g., social life/cultural values）, and it teaches us that + 道理.

例：Oliver Twist reflects the hard life of poor people in 1800s London, and it teaches us that kindness can change lives19 .The Old Man and the Sea reflects the spirit of never

giving up, and it teaches us that a man can be destroyed but not defeated12.（该句型贴合文档中对经典书籍 “现实意义” 的强调，如《雾都孤儿》对社会生活的反映、《老人与海》传递的精神，符合 Section B “从好书中学习” 的主题 8 。）

10. 描述读书报告的组成部分：A good book report should include + 组成部分（e.g., basic information, plot summary, personal opinion）, because + 理由.

例：A good book report should include the book’s title, writer and type, because readers need basic information first.A good book report should include your personal opinion,

because it shows what you’ve learned from the book.（该句型直接对应文档中 “撰写读书报告” 的要求，文档明确指出读书报告需包含基本信息、情节总结、个人观点等，如 Section B 3a 对读书报告结构的分析。）

五、重点语法

（一）现在完成时（Present Perfect Tense）

1. 核心用法

. 描述 过 去 阅读 经 历 对 现 在 的 影 响： 强 调 “ 是 否读 过 ”“ 是 否 有 收 获 ” ， 常 与already/yet/never/ever/just 连用，是单元语法核心。

例：Have you ever read The Three-Body Problem? — No, I’ve never heard of it, but Fu Xing says it’s interesting.Chen Jie has already borrowed Around the World in Eighty

Days from the library, but she hasn’t read it yet.（文档中 Section A 的对话、Grammar Focus 均围绕该用法展开，如 Peter 与 Fu Xing 讨论书籍阅读情况，明确要求区分“已读 / 未读” 的状态 4 。）

. 描述从过去持续到现在的阅读习惯 / 状态：常与 since/for 连用，体现 “长期阅读”或 “持续影响”。

例：Yaming has loved fantasy novels since he read Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone.Volunteer 3 has been a beach clean-up volunteer for seven years, and she often

reads books about environmental protection in her free time.（文档中 “现在完成时与 since/for 连用” 是语法拓展点，贴合 “长期阅读习惯”“持续志愿经历” 等场景，如Section B 对志愿者阅读爱好的描述。）

2. 句式结构与标志词

句式类型

结构

文档例句

肯定句

主语 + have/has +过去分词

I’ve just finished Alice ,s Adventures in Wonderland.

否定句

主语 + haven’t/hasn’t + 过去分词

Peter hasn’t read Alice ,s Adventures in Wonderland yet.

一般疑问句

Have/Has + 主语 +过去分词

Has Yaming read all the Harry Potter books? — Yes, he has.

特殊疑问句

疑问词 + have/has +主语 + 过去分词

What books have you read recently? — I’ve read The Romance ofthe Three

Kingdoms.

. 标志词用法：

. already（已完成，用于肯定句）、yet（未完成，用于否定句 / 疑问句）、never（从未，表否定）、ever（曾经，用于疑问句）、just（刚刚，表近期完成）。

例：I’ve already read The Little Match Girl many times5 .Have you ever heard of Outlaws

of the Marsh?

3. 与一般过去时的辨析

. 现在完成时：强调 “过去动作与现在的关联”（如是否有收获、是否持续），不与具体过去时间状语（yesterday/last week）连用；

. 一般过去时：仅描述 “过去发生的动作”（如过去何时读某本书），与具体过去时间状语连用。

例：Fu Xing read Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland last month.（一般过去时，仅说明“上个月读了”，不涉及现在）1.Fu Xing has read Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland, so she can talk about it with Peter.（现在完成时，强调 “已读过，能交流” 的现在影响）

（二）动词不定式（Infinitives）在读书报告中的应用

1. 作目的状语

. 用法：表示 “撰写读书报告、推荐书籍” 的目的，常译为 “为了 … …” ，贯穿单元写作任务。

例：We need to write a summary of the story to list the main events14 .I recommend The Secret Garden to you to help you understand friendship.

2. 作宾语补足语

. 用法：用于 “推荐、建议” 类动词后，补充说明宾语的动作，常见动词有 tell, ask, recommend, encourage 等。

例：The teacher asked us to write a book report about a classic novel.Fu Xing encouraged

Peter to read Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland.

3. “疑问词 + 不定式” 结构

. 用法：用于 “询问读书相关的方法、选择” ，如 “如何写读书报告”“选哪本书” ，常作宾语。

例：I don’t know how to write a good book report—can you help me?She asked me which book to choose for her report.

（三）连词 so...that（结果状语从句）

1. 核心用法

. 表示 “书籍特征导致的阅读结果”：so 后接形容词（如 interesting/mysterious），that后接结果从句，体现书籍对读者的吸引力。

例：The story of The Secret Garden is so warm that it makes many readers cry7 .The Final Problem is so exciting that I finished it in one sitting18.（文档中 Grammar 部分明确该用法，结合《秘密花园》《最后一案》等书籍案例，要求用 so...that 描述阅读感受 29。）

2. 句式变换

. 肯定式：主语 + be + so + 形容词 + that + 从句

例：The book is so popular that it has been translated into many languages.

. 否定式：主语 + be + not so + 形容词 + that + 从句

例：This novel is not so difficult that you can’t understand it—just take your time.

（四）名词与动词同形的词汇（文档词汇重点）

1. 单元高频词汇及用法

. 常见词汇：attack（v. 攻击 /n. 攻击）、report（v. 报告 /n. 报告）、state（v. 陈述 /n.状态）、base（v. 以 … … 为基础 /n. 基础）。

例：In The Three-Body Problem, aliens plan to attack the Earth.（v. 攻击）.The attack from aliens makes the story exciting.（n. 攻击）.She will report on The Old Man and the Sea in class.（v. 报告）12.Her book report is full of interesting ideas.（n. 报告）

2. 用法注意

. 需根据语境判断词性：作动词时，后接宾语或状语；作名词时，可作主语、宾语或定语（如 book report）。

例：The writer based his story on real life.（v. 以 … … 为基础，后接宾语 his story） The base of the story is a true event.（n. 基础，作主语）
