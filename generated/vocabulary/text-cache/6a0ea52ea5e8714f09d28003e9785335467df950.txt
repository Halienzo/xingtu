Unit 3 Same or Different
一、语法考点
（一）形容词和副词的比较级
1. 构成规则
①一般在词尾加 -er。例如：tall - taller，fast - faster。
②以字母 e 结尾的词，加 -r。例如：nice - nicer，fine - finer。
③重读闭音节词词尾只有一个辅音字母时，先双写这个辅音字母，再加 -er。
例如：big - bigger，hot - hotter。
④以 “辅音字母 + y” 结尾的双音节词，先把 y 改为 i，再加 -er。例如：
happy - happier，early - earlier。
⑤多音节词和部分双音节词在前面加 more。例如：interesting - more
interesting，outgoing - more outgoing。
2. 用法：表示两者之间的比较，常用 “比较级 + than” 结构。例如：My sister is taller
than me.（我姐姐比我高。）
3. 特殊句型
①“as + 形容词 / 副词原级 + as” 表示 “和…… 一样”。例如：Tom is as tall
as Jack.（汤姆和杰克一样高。）
②“not as/so + 形容词 / 副词原级 + as” 表示 “不如……”。例如：This book
is not as interesting as that one.（这本书不如那本书有趣。）
③“比较级 + and + 比较级” 表示 “越来越……”。例如：It's getting colder
and colder.（天气越来越冷了。）
④“the + 比较级，the + 比较级” 表示 “越……，越……”。例如：The more you
read, the more you know.（你读得越多，知道得就越多。）
（二）选择疑问句
1. 结构：一般疑问句 + or + 选择部分？例如：Do you like apples or bananas?（你
喜欢苹果还是香蕉？）

2. 回答：不能用 yes 或 no 回答，需从提供的选项中选择其一进行回答。例如：
I like apples.（我喜欢苹果。）
二、词汇考点
（一）重点单词
1. outgoing：adj. 爱交际的；友好的；外向的。比较级为 more outgoing。例如：
She is more outgoing than her sister.（她比她姐姐更外向。）
2. better：adj. & adv. 较好的（地）；更好的（地），是 good/well 的比较级。
例如：This idea is better.（这个主意更好。）He plays basketball better than me.
（他篮球打得比我好。）
3. loudly：adv. 喧闹地；大声地；响亮地。比较级为 more loudly。例如：Please
don't speak so loudly.（请不要这么大声说话。）
4. quietly：adv. 轻声地；轻柔地；安静地。比较级为 more quietly。例如：The baby
is sleeping. Please talk quietly.（宝宝在睡觉，请轻声说话。）
5. hard - working：adj. 工作努力的；辛勤的。比较级为 more hard - working。
例如：My classmates are all hard - working.（我的同学们都很勤奋。）
6. competition：n. 比赛；竞赛；竞争。例如：We will have a singing competition
next week.（下周我们将举行一场歌唱比赛。）
7. fantastic：adj. 极好的；了不起的。例如：We had a fantastic time at the party.
（我们在聚会上玩得很开心。）
8. which：pron. & adj. 哪一个；哪一些。例如：Which book do you like best?（你
最喜欢哪本书？）
9. clearly：adv. 清楚地；清晰地；明白地。比较级为 more clearly。例如：Speak
clearly so that everyone can hear you.（说清楚点，以便每个人都能听到你。）
10. win：v. 获胜；赢；赢得。过去式为 won。例如：Our team won the game.（我
们队赢了这场比赛。）
（二）重点短语
1. care about：关心；在意。例如：We should care about our friends.（我们应该
关心我们的朋友。）
2. be different from：与…… 不同。例如：My lifestyle is different from yours.（我
的生活方式和你的不同。）

-- 2 of 12 --

3. as long as：只要；既然。例如：You can get good grades as long as you study
hard.（只要你努力学习，你就能取得好成绩。）
4. bring out：使显现；使表现出。例如：This kind of music can bring out the best
in her.（这种音乐能激发出她最好的一面。）
5. the same as：和…… 相同；与…… 一致。例如：His bike is the same as mine.
（他的自行车和我的一样。）

1. My brother is ________ than me. He likes talking with others.
A. outgoing B. more outgoing C. most outgoing D. the most outgoing
答案：B
解析：由 than 可知用比较级，outgoing 是多音节词，其比较级是 more
outgoing，所以选 B。
2. — Who sings ________ in your class?
— Li Ming does.
A. better B. best C. good D. well
答案：B
解析：in your class 表示范围，在班级里比较唱歌情况，用最高级，sing 是
动词，用副词 well 的最高级 best 修饰，所以选 B。

答案：A
解析：“as + 形容词原级 + as” 表示 “和…… 一样”，所以用 tall 的原级，
选 A。

In modern society, people are becoming more and more concerned about
their health. There are different ways to maintain a healthy lifestyle, and each
person may choose the one that suits them best.
Tom is a 15 - year - old middle school student. He likes playing basketball
very much. He plays basketball with his classmates after school three times a
week. He believes that playing basketball not only helps him keep fit but also
improves his teamwork skills. He also eats a balanced diet. He always has a
glass of milk and an egg for breakfast. He eats plenty of fruits and vegetables
every day. He hardly ever eats junk food like hamburgers and chips.
On the other hand, Lily has a different way to stay healthy. She is very
interested in yoga. She goes to a yoga class twice a week. Yoga makes her
body more flexible and helps her relax. She also pays attention to her sleep.
She goes to bed before 10 o'clock every night and sleeps for about 8 hours.
She thinks a good sleep is very important for her health.
Another student, Jack, likes running. He runs for half an hour every
morning. Running makes him energetic throughout the day. He also tries to
reduce his stress. He listens to music when he is tired, which helps him calm
down.

In conclusion, there are various ways to live a healthy life. Whether it's
through sports, a balanced diet, good sleep, or stress - reduction methods,
everyone can find their own path to health.
