汤姆在哪里？---He has gone to Shanghai. 他已经去了上海。2. has been to：表示去过某地，（现已回来）（不可与for+一段时间连用）。例：I have been to New York three times so far. 到目前为止，我已经去过纽约三次了。3. has been in：表示一直呆在某地，常与时间段搭配。例：I have been in the army for more than 5 years.我在部队已经呆了五年多了。题模一：基本用法例1.1.1  I _________ already _________ in this school for three years.A．  was; studyingB．  are; studyingC．  will; studyD．  have; studied例1.1.2  I _________ English since 2010.A．  have learntB．  have learnC．  learntD．  learn例1.1.3  I _________ my pencil yet.A．  have findB．  haven’t foundC．  foundD．  find例1.1.4  Mr. Brown isn’t at home.
